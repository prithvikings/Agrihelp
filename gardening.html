Sustainable Gardening Tips

Expert advice to help you grow a thriving, eco-friendly garden

Soil Health

Start Composting

Turn kitchen scraps into nutrient-rich compost. Layer greens (vegetable scraps) and browns (dry leaves) in a 1:3 ratio.

Water Saving

Smart Watering Techniques

Water early in the morning to reduce evaporation. Use drip irrigation or soaker hoses for efficient water delivery.

Pest Control

Natural Pest Deterrents

Plant marigolds to repel nematodes and attract ladybugs. Use neem oil spray for common garden pests.

Soil Health

The Power of Mulching

Apply 2-3 inches of organic mulch to retain moisture, suppress weeds, and regulate soil temperature.

Plant Selection

Choose Native Plants

Native plants require less water and maintenance while supporting local wildlife and pollinators.

Vegetable Gardening

Practice Crop Rotation

Rotate vegetable families each season to prevent soil depletion and reduce disease buildup.
